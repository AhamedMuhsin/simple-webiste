Quick Chocolate Mug Cake

A 2-minute single-serve dessert you can make in the microwave — perfect for instant cravings.

Ingredients

Serves 1
  • 4 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 3 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder
  • 3 tbsp milk
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1/4 tsp vanilla extract (optional)
  • a pinch of salt

Tip: Stir until smooth to avoid dry pockets.

Steps

  1. Combine dry ingredients in a microwave-safe mug.
  2. Add milk, oil, and vanilla — stir until smooth.
  3. Microwave on high for 60–90 seconds (time depends on wattage).
  4. Let cool 1 minute, then enjoy directly from the mug.

Notes & Variations

Make it fudgier: add 1 tbsp of chocolate chips. Make it vegan: use plant milk and flax oil.

Nutrition (approx)

~350 kcal • 10g fat • 45g carbs
